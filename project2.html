Funova

Funova is a next-generation multiplayer gaming platform where users can challenge friends or compete globally in real-time wager games — including classics like UNO, Chess, Horse Race, Snake, and more. Designed for both casual players and competitive enthusiasts, Funova blends entertainment with engagement through sleek animations, intuitive controls, and a fast, responsive interface.

The app features secure wallet integrations, matchmaking systems, and in-game chat to create an immersive social experience. Built using Flutter and Firebase for real-time performance across devices, Funova reimagines how mobile gaming brings people together — fun, competitive, and reward-driven.
